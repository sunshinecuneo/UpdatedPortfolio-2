About Me

I am a single mom to a wonderfully precocious, very happy nine year old beauty whose name is Grace! We have a smiley dog named Mavis and three chickens; Murdoch, April and Little Peepers. I live in Denver in the house I grew up in. I am a fiercely indepedant introvert who loves most to be at home enjoying time with my daughter and our little urban farm family. I enjoy sleeping, watching tv, gardening and cooking and baking, especially for others. I can fix or construct just about anything. I have a very strong intuition that I trust completely.

I have a strong work ethic; often going above and beyond in all I do. I was taught that if I'm going to do a job I better do it right so I strive for accuracy. I am dedicated and reliable and I can efficiently multitask. I have great research skills and think of myself as highly resourceful. I can work collaboratively as part of a team or individually with minimal supervision.
